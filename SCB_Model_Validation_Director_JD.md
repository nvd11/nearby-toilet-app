# Standard Chartered Bank - Job Description
## 职位名称：模型验证总监 (Head of Model Validation Team)
**汇报对象:** Executive Director, AI Safety & AI Talent (CDO office)
**工作地点:** 广州
**经验要求:** 10年以上 (FSI domain), 5+ years in model validation/risk/data science.
**学历要求:** 本科

## 职位详情 (Job Summary)
负责评估机器学习 (ML)、数据科学和生成式 AI (GenAI) 模型，确保符合最新技术进展、监管要求和既定的模型风险管理 (MRM) 框架。主导开发和实施强大的 AI 和非 AI 模型验证方法，确保整个组织模型组合的合规性、准确性和可靠性。

## 关键职责 (Key Responsibilities)
- **Leadership & Strategy:** Lead AI model validation, fostering technical excellence and regulatory compliance. Develop strategic vision for validation incorporating ML, DS, and GenAI trends.
- **Collaboration & Governance:** Collaborate with data science, risk management, and compliance for cohesive model governance.
- **Validation Frameworks:** Design/implement frameworks for ML/DS/GenAI leveraging explainable AI, fairness metrics, and robustness testing. Ensure alignment with MRM practices for non-AI models, addressing AI challenges (black-box, data drift, ethics).
- **Standards & Regulation:** Develop policies/procedures. Integrate global regulatory requirements (SR 11-7, Basel, GDPR, EU AI Act).
- **Performance Evaluation:** Oversee evaluation of accuracy, stability, scalability, fairness (stress testing, backtesting, sensitivity analysis). Assess interpretability/explainability.
- **Data & Bias:** Monitor data quality, feature engineering, preprocessing pipelines to mitigate bias.
- **Risk Management:** Integrate MRM principles (inventory management, risk tiering). Mitigate operational, ethical, reputational risks.
- **Reporting:** Prepare/present reports to senior management, auditors, regulators.

## 任职资格 (Qualifications)
- 10+ years in FSI (Financial Services Industry) domain.
- 5+ years in model validation, risk management, or data science.
- Deep expertise in ML, DS, GenAI (TensorFlow, PyTorch, NLP, Computer Vision).
- Strong understanding of MRM frameworks & regulations (SR 11-7, OCC, Basel, EU AI Act).
- Leadership, complex project management, senior stakeholder engagement.
- Excellent communication (translating technical findings to actionable insights).
- Tech Stack: Python, R. Validation tools (SHAP, LIME, Fairlearn).
